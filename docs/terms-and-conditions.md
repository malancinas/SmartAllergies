## 2. Description of the App

Local Allergies is a personal allergy and pollen tracking application. It provides:

- Real-time and forecast pollen and air quality data based on your location;

## 8. Statistical Model — Accuracy and Limitations

### 8.1 How the Model Works

The Pro "Personal Allergen Model" uses statistical methods (Pearson correlation and ordinary least-squares regression) applied to **your own symptom logs and the environmental data captured alongside them**. All computation is performed **entirely on your device**. The model is not trained on data from other users, and your personal data is not uploaded to any server for model computation.

### 8.2 Accuracy Limitations

The statistical outputs produced by the allergen model are **indicative only** and subject to significant limitations:

- The model requires a minimum number of logged data points to produce meaningful results (7 days for basic, 14 days for advanced). Results from small datasets may be unreliable.
- Correlation does not imply causation. A statistical association between a pollen type and your symptom severity does not confirm that the pollen type causes your symptoms.
- The model cannot account for lifestyle variables not captured in the App (e.g. diet, stress, time spent outdoors, indoor air quality, pet exposure).
- Environmental data is fetched from third-party APIs and reflects modelled or measured values for your approximate location; it may not precisely reflect the pollen conditions you were actually exposed to.
- The medications field is a free-text log and is not used in any statistical computation in the current version of the App.
- Model results may change significantly as you log more data; early results in particular should be treated with caution.

### 8.3 Not a Diagnostic Tool

The model outputs do not constitute a clinical allergen test, a diagnosis of allergy to any substance, or a recommendation to avoid any substance. For a clinical allergen assessment, seek testing from a qualified allergist.
